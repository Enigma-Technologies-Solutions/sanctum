# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Be respectful and constructive. Specifically:

- Welcome people regardless of background or experience level.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Keep discussions on-topic.

Not acceptable:
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Deliberately derailing technical discussions.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to [ultra@enigma.sh](mailto:ultra@enigma.sh).  
Reports are reviewed within 48 hours. Repeat or severe violations result in a
permanent ban from the project.

## Scope

Applies in all project spaces: GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, and any
official communication channels.
